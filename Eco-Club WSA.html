Who are we; the WSA Eco-Club

The WSA Eco-Club is an organisation formed by Ms Naazneen, the Head Of Sustainability on a mission to reduce our Carbon Footprint. Our team consists of Teachers and Srudents from different Year Groups working together owards a common goal. Our main Sustainability Leader's come from Year 6, 7, 8 and 9. They are Maham Talha, Ameer Hashim, Abullah Hamid and Odira . These 4 are the main representatives for our Eco-Club.


What have we done to achieve our main goal??

The WSA Eco-Club has organised a Tree-Plantation Drive as a small step towards a brighter future. We will start small to achieve big, and follow with activities that will lead to a great change! We aim to get the Green Flag which is a flag that is given to schools worldwide to show that we are a sustainable school. Previously it was easy to get, but as the climate get's worse and pollution increases, they expect more from us, but together, we can do anything. And our goal is to obtain the GREEN FLAG
